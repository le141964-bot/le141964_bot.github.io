New York Times columnist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as "the most famous financial journalist of his generation." A leading voice on Wall Street and corporate America, his New York Times bestseller, Too Big to Fail, was the first true, behind-the-scenes account of how the financial crisis developed into a global tsunami.

The Economist, The Financial Times, and Business Week all named Too Big to Fail one of the best books of the year. The book was published by Viking October 20, 2009. The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premiered on HBO on May 23, 2011.

Praise for Too Big to Fail

"Vigorously reported, superbly organized...for those of us who didn’t pursue MBAs and have the penny-ante salaries to prove it." Julia Keller, Chicago Tribune
"Sorkin’s prodigious reporting and lively writing put the reader in the room for some of the biggest-dollar conference calls in history. It’s an entertaining, brisk book." Paul M. Barrett, The New York Times Book Review
